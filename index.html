FAQ

Do buyers still need a licensed broker?

Local rules vary. Jonafin streamlines offer submission and fee transparency — licensed broker involvement depends on state law and seller requirements.

How are fees handled?

Fee splits are disclosed up front. Jonafin does not add hidden brokerage premiums — the platform enables transparent arrangements between buyer, seller, and broker.

Is Jonafin available everywhere?

Not yet. We roll out markets gradually to ensure compliance and quality. Enter your location to join waitlists.

Is my data private?

We protect data and only share offer details with parties needed to process the transaction. See our privacy policy for full details (not included in this demo page).
